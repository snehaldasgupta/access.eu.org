What's Camel Case and why should I be using it?

Camel Case is the practice of capitalising the first letter of each word when writing compound words or phrases in a hashtag.

It allows screen readers to identify individual words in a hashtag. This makes hashtags accessible to blind and visually impaired people, as well as people with dyslexia.

Camel case improves understanding and readability for everyone. This helps people in a hurry, or people with low English fluency.
