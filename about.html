Youth Initiative for Accessibility

Kindness, Equality, and Accessibility
The Internet is designed to work for all, regardless of hardware, software, language, or ability. This necessitates the design of accessible web content, taking into account people with disabilities and impairments. However, currently most of the web is developed with accessibility barriers that make them difficult or impossible for some people to use. Making the web accessible benefits individuals, businesses, and society.
